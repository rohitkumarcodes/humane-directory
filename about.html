About this projects

The big-tech internet feels crowded, cold, and chaotic. On the other hand, personal websites feel wholesome, warm, and humane. Often, I find a new personal website and it feels like I've found a new miniature world that I can stroll around in.

It took me years to collect a few dozen websites that are text-oriented, updated regularly, have RSS/Atom feeds. I've made this directory to make it easier for others to find such personal websites.

PS: I am not looking for submissions. However, if you want to share a personal website or just want to say hello, feel free to email me at hello@rohit.online

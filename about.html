About This Project

The wide internet feels crowded, cold, and chaotic. On the other hand, personal websites feel wholesome, warm, and humane. Often, I find a new personal website and it feels like I've found a new miniature world that I can stroll around in.

It took me years to collect a few dozen websites that are text-oriented, updated regularly, have RSS/Atom feeds. I thought make a directory that would make it easier for others to find such personal websites. There are other directories but I feel they are not pruned often enough. Hence, I made my own directory.

PS: I am not looking for submissions. However, if you want to share a personal website or just want to say hello, feel free to email me at hello@rohit.online
